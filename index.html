The Story

How They Met

  • The First We Met

    August, 2010

    August 2010 was the first day of College for Bia in Bandung Institute of Technology. One of the things she should do as a freshman was choose one student club. She decided to join Japanese Culture Club since she is interested in Japanese language. There she met a popular, 2 years older senpai. Her heart skipped a beat when their eyes met. She had feeling toward him but too shy to say a thing and decided to hide her feeling away. (senpai: senior)

  • Love is in The Air

    14 March 2013

    She never knew that all this time senpai has the same feeling. Until finally, on white day, senpai expressed his feeling and gave her a white chocolate bar which inside was hidden a silver ring. Since then, they committed to always be together and take care of each other.

  • One Step Closer

    8 July 2017

    Even though, like a normal relationship, they had to face many obstacles, but their love is greater and stronger than that. From the start they are serious with each other. They did their best to prepare for the next step. After 4 more years together, they got engaged.

  • The Beginning of New Journey

    3 December 2017

    Finally the long-awaited day has come. They say holy promises and become husband and wife. After the marriage, they live in a small house on the edge of Bandung city and start a new chapter of their life.
