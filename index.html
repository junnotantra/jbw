The Story

  • Our First Encounter

    August, 2010

    August 2010 was Bia’s first month in Bandung Institute of Technology. As a freshman, one of the things she ought to do was to choose at least one student club. She decided to join the Japanese Cultural Club with her interest in the country’s language. There, she met a popular senpai (Japanese word for “senior”) who is 2 years older than her. Her heart skipped a beat when her eyes met his. She harboured a special feeling towards him but was too bashful to express it. At the end, she decided to lock her feeling away, telling nobody but herself.

  • Love Is in the Air

    14 March 2013

    What lied in that senpai’s heart was unbeknown to her. Until on 14 March 2017, the day of the White Day, the senpai expressed her his feeling. He gave her a bar of white chocolate, and hidden inside it was a silver ring. She never knew that after all this time, the senpai has the same feeling. Since that day, they commit to be always together and take care of each other, through happiness and hard time.

  • One Step Closer

    8 July 2017

    Like any other relationships, they were faced with many obstacles. But they know and believe that their love for each other is stronger and would not die down from the difficulties. They hold on to their commitment made 4 years ago and did their best to advance to the next step: they got engaged.

  • The Start of a New Journey

    3 December 2017

    Finally, the long-awaited day has come. The holy promise is declared and they become husband and wife, After their marriage, they move to a small house on the outskirt of Bandung and start a new chapter of their life. Together forever.
